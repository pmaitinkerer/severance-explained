SEPARATION AGREEMENT AND GENERAL RELEASE

This Separation Agreement is entered into between Meridian Systems Inc. ("the Company") and the Employee. The Employee's last day of employment will be June 30, 2026.

Severance Benefits: The Company will pay the Employee the equivalent of six (6) weeks of base salary, less applicable withholdings, paid in a lump sum within 30 days of the Effective Date. The Company will also pay the employer portion of COBRA premiums for two (2) months following the separation date.

Release of Claims: In exchange for the severance benefits, the Employee agrees to release and waive any and all claims against the Company, including but not limited to claims arising under federal, state, and local employment laws, whether known or unknown, up to the date of signing.

Non-Competition: For a period of twelve (12) months following the separation date, the Employee agrees not to engage in or be employed by any business that competes with the Company within the United States.

Non-Disparagement: The Employee agrees not to make any disparaging statements about the Company at any time.

Acceptance: This offer must be signed and returned within five (5) business days. If not returned by that date, the offer is withdrawn.
