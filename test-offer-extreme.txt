SEPARATION AGREEMENT AND COMPLETE RELEASE OF ALL CLAIMS

This Agreement is between Apex Dynamics LLC ("Company") and the Employee. Last day of employment: immediately upon signing.

Severance: The Company will pay the Employee a total of $500.00, less applicable deductions, contingent upon the Employee first signing this Agreement and completing a 90-day unpaid "transition consulting period" during which the Employee remains available to the Company up to 40 hours per week.

Release of Claims: The Employee waives any and all claims, known or unknown, including claims under the Age Discrimination in Employment Act, Title VII, the Family and Medical Leave Act, workers' compensation claims, and any claims for unpaid wages or overtime. The Employee also waives the right to file or participate in any charge with the EEOC, NLRB, or any government agency.

Non-Competition: For five (5) years following separation, the Employee shall not work in any industry, in any capacity, anywhere in the world.

Non-Disparagement: The Employee shall never make any statement, public or private, regarding the Company. Violation incurs liquidated damages of $250,000 per occurrence.

Cooperation: The Employee agrees to repay all severance plus the Company's attorney fees if the Employee challenges any provision of this Agreement.

Confidentiality: The Employee may not disclose the existence or terms of this Agreement to anyone, including a spouse or attorney.

Acceptance: This offer must be signed within 24 hours. By signing, the Employee affirms they were given adequate time to consult counsel.
